Plov Recipe

Home

Description

Plov, also known as pilaf or palov, is a hearty and aromatic rice dish considered the national dish of Uzbekistan and a staple throughout Central Asia, Iran, and Afghanistan.

Ingredients

  • Long-grain rice
  • Lamb meat
  • Carrots
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients: Cut meat, slice carrots into matchsticks, and dice onions. Rinse rice thoroughly until water runs clear
  2. Heat oil in a Dutch oven or large pot and sear the meat until browned.
  3. Add onions and carrots, saut√©ing until softened. Add spices like cumin and coriander.
  4. Add water or broth and let the mixture simmer to create the "zirvak" base.
  5. Layer the rinsed rice over the meat and vegetables without stirring. Add more water to cover the rice.
  6. Insert a whole head of garlic into the rice, then cover the pot and simmer on low heat until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Mix the ingredients before serving.
